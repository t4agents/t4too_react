Account Deletion Request

Last updated: April 8, 2026

This page explains how users of the mobile application T4Agents AI Payroll App (package name: com.teamofone.t4agents), operated by T4Agents.com, can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your account and associated data, please send an email to:

t4agents@google.com

Please include the following information in your request:

  • The email address associated with your account
  • A clear statement that you wish to delete your account

Data That Will Be Deleted

Upon verification of your request, we will delete:

  • Your account and authentication data
  • Your saved preferences and favorites (stored via Supabase)
  • Any user-specific data associated with your account

Data That May Be Retained

Certain data may be retained for a limited period for legitimate purposes, including:

  • Security, fraud prevention, and abuse detection
  • Legal obligations and compliance
  • Aggregated, anonymized analytics data (which cannot identify you)

Retention Period

Account deletion requests are processed within a reasonable timeframe. Residual data may be retained for up to 30 days for backup and system integrity purposes, after which it is permanently deleted.

Contact

If you have any questions about account deletion, please contact: t4agents@google.com
